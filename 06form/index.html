About the Artist

Hello, I am a Native America:Navajo artist. I am from Farmington, New Mexico. Currently located in Utah Valley studying Graphic Design. I have tried an assortment of art styles and mediums throughout middle school to highschool. I recently decided my favorite is digital media. I love digital media because of how easily I get lost in it. I am a chill laid back introvert that loves to read or watch kdramas with my two dogs.

Watercolor is one of my favorite subjects, but the most difficult and challenging style for me. Watercolor is euphoric and peaceul style I love.

Why I Chose to Pursue Art

  • I had amazing art teachers throughout middle school and high school that inspired me to pursue art as a degree.
  • For as long as I could remember, I loved drawing andd as a kid I would always draw on napkins or anything I could get my hands on.
  • I took a digital media class in highschool that helped me realize there are a lot of careers in art that are expanding and growing.
